# 05 — Accessibility as a first-class gate

## Shot list

1. **00:00 — Cold open.** A red "WCAG AA breach" message in the QA
   dashboard.
2. **00:45 — Why a11y in QA?** 57% of issues catchable automatically.
3. **02:00 — Vendoring axe-core.** Place axe.min.js under
   `docs/nexus/vendor/`; show `InjectionScript(assetBase)`.
4. **04:00 — Parse + Assert.** Parse a captured JSON; call
   `Assert(LevelAA)`; show the typed error listing the rule ids.
5. **06:00 — Section 508.** Filter with `Section508()`; export the
   subset as a compliance report.
6. **08:00 — Fail the build.** Integrate the assertion into a bank
   case; break the UI; watch HelixQA fail the step with a clear
   message.
7. **10:00 — Outro.** AAA vs AA decisions.
